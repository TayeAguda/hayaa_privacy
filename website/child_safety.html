Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

DO-DEV, the developer of the Hayaa mobile application ("Application"), is deeply committed to child safety. This page outlines our standards and practices to prevent, detect, and respond to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Hayaa has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward any content or behavior that sexually exploits or abuses minors. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) in any form;
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors;
  • Any content that sexualizes individuals under the age of 18.

Any user found engaging in such behavior will be immediately banned and reported to the relevant authorities.

2. Age Restrictions

Hayaa is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. We require users to confirm their age during the registration process. Accounts suspected of belonging to minors are subject to immediate suspension pending verification.

3. Reporting Child Safety Issues

We provide users with clear and accessible ways to report child safety concerns directly within the Application:

  • In-app reporting: Users can report any profile, message, or content they believe involves a minor or constitutes CSAE by using the "Report" feature available on every profile and conversation screen.
  • Email reporting: Users may also contact our safety team directly at ilhamhidayatul122@gmail.com with the subject line "Child Safety Report".

All reports are treated with the utmost urgency and confidentiality.

4. How We Handle Reports

Upon receiving a child safety report, our process is as follows:

  • The reported account and content are immediately reviewed by our safety team;
  • The reported account may be suspended during the review process;
  • If CSAM or CSAE is confirmed, the content is removed immediately and the account is permanently banned;
  • We report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline as required by law;
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations.

5. Proactive Safety Measures

In addition to responding to reports, we take proactive steps to protect minors:

  • Age verification during account registration;
  • Monitoring for suspicious patterns of behavior that may indicate grooming or exploitation;
  • Regular review of our platform safety practices to align with industry best standards;
  • User education on safe online behavior within the Application.

We take every report seriously and will respond promptly.
